# **App Name**: Моята Бременност А-Я

## Core Features:

- Immersive Hero Section: Showcases the book title, a compelling subheadline, an emotional paragraph, discounted pricing with a CTA, and a calming visual to immediately engage visitors.
- Persuasive Content Journey: Guides the user through problem identification, unique solutions offered by the book, and compelling benefits, all presented with emotionally resonant Bulgarian copywriting.
- Strategic Pricing Display: Highlights the limited-time launch price prominently in multiple sections (top bar, hero, dedicated section) with clear 'crossed-out' standard price and urgency messaging.
- Interactive Book Overview: Presents key learnings through an icon-based list and a detailed chapter preview to give visitors a clear understanding of the book's value.
- Dynamic Testimonial Integration: Displays social proof through impactful placeholder testimonials that reinforce trust and the book's positive influence on other mothers.
- Responsive & Mobile-First Design: Ensures the entire landing page layout and interactive elements are optimized for seamless viewing and conversion across all devices, especially mobile.
- Interactive FAQ Section: Addresses common questions about the book in an expandable format, providing quick clarity and reducing purchase friction for prospective buyers.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary action color: A soft, muted rosy pink (#CC667F) for CTAs, highlighted text, and key interactive elements, reflecting warmth and femininity while ensuring visibility against the light background.
- Background color: A serene, barely-there creamy white with a hint of warmth (#F7F1F2), providing a clean, spacious, and calming canvas for the content.
- Accent color: A gentle, muted lavender (#DDC8DE) for secondary highlights, subtle decorative elements, and complementary icons, creating a sophisticated analogous palette.
- Headline font: 'Alegreya' (serif) for an elegant, intellectual, and contemporary feel that draws the reader in.
- Body text font: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for high readability, modern appeal, and a touch of warmth in longer content sections.
- Use rounded, line-based or subtly filled icons that complement the soft and minimalist design aesthetic, employing muted pastel colors from the palette.
- Features generous whitespace, gently rounded UI elements, and soft shadows to convey elegance, calmness, and a modern, approachable design.
- Incorporate subtle background animations, such as soft cloud or cotton smoke effects with white and light pink tones, to enhance the calming and dreamy atmosphere, and minimal, elegant hover states for interactive elements to guide user attention.
